# Authority Flywheel Growth Roadmap (12 Months)

## Phase 1: Foundation & "Parasite" Seeds (Months 1-3)
- **Goal**: Establish indexed pages for high-volume "vs" and "price" keywords.
- **Action**:
    - Manually curate top 50 guitars/synths.
    - Run Video Harvester on top 20 creators.
    - Launch `/price-history` pages for these 50 items.
    - **KPI**: 1,000 indexed pages.

## Phase 2: The Creator Circular Loop (Months 4-6)
- **Goal**: Rank for "[creator] gear" queries.
- **Action**:
    - Expand Video Harvester to top 100 creators.
    - Auto-generate `/gear-used-by-[creator]` pages.
    - Reach out to smaller creators to "claim" their page (backlink opportunity).
    - **KPI**: 500 daily organic visitors.

## Phase 3: Programmatic Scale (Months 7-9)
- **Goal**: domination of long-tail queries.
- **Action**:
    - Activate the Programmatic SEO Engine for:
        - `/best-[instrument]-under-[price]`
        - `/best-[instrument]-for-[genre]`
    - Ingest affiliate feeds from Thomann/Amazon to expand catalog to 10k products.
    - **KPI**: 5,000 indexed pages, 2,000 daily visitors.

## Phase 4: Community & AI (Months 10-12)
- **Goal**: Retention and User Data.
- **Action**:
    - Launch "Build My Studio" AI Assistant.
    - Allow users to "Save to My Studio" (requires Auth).
    - Email loops for price alerts on tracked gear.
    - **KPI**: 10% Returning User Rate, Affiliate Revenue Consistency.
